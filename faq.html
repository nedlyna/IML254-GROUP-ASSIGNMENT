FAQ - GENERAL QUESTION

Q1: What makes your cookies soft?.

A1: Our cookies are soft because we use a unique blend of high-quality ingredients, including fresh butter, brown sugar, and a hint of cornstarch. This combination keeps our cookies moist and chewy.

Q2: Are your cookies made from scratch?

A2: Yes, all our cookies are made from scratch daily in our bakery using premium, all-natural ingredients.

Q3: Do you offer gluten-free options?

A3: Yes, we offer a selection of gluten-free cookies. Please check our menu or ask our staff for more details.

Q4: Are your cookies vegan?

A4: We have a variety of vegan cookies available. Our vegan cookies are made without any animal products.

Q5: How should I store the cookies to keep them soft?

A5: To keep your cookies soft, store them in an airtight container at room temperature. You can also add a slice of bread to the container to help maintain moisture.
